WHY ASME ?


ASME SRM Students section is a student-run section for ASME in SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur. We are a group of undergraduate students from Mechanical Engineering, aimed at promoting the practise of mechanical and multidisciplinary Engineering. We also conduct differewnt technical events, industrial visits, conferences and Seminars for the students of various disciplines.
